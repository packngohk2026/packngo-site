接仔 PackNGo

外傭活動協調，輕鬆搞定 · Helper Activity Coordinator

關於 About

接仔 PackNGo 是一款專為香港家庭設計的應用程式，協助家長與外傭姐姐輕鬆協調子女的日常課外活動安排，包括出發提醒、打包清單及接送通知。支援中文、菲律賓文及印尼文。

PackNGo is designed for Hong Kong families to coordinate children's extracurricular activities between parents and domestic helpers — covering departure reminders, packing checklists, and pickup notifications. Available in Traditional Chinese, Filipino (Tagalog), and Indonesian.

常見問題 FAQ

  • 如何新增活動？ 家長登入後，點選「新增活動」，填寫活動名稱、時間及打包物品即可。
  • 外傭如何收到通知？ 外傭姐姐需在設定中完成語言選擇，系統會自動以其選擇的語言發送提醒。
  • 支援哪些語言？ 繁體中文、英文、菲律賓文 (Tagalog)、印尼文 (Bahasa Indonesia)。
  • How do I add an activity? Log in as a parent, tap "Add Activity", and fill in the name, time, and packing items.
  • How does the helper receive notifications? The helper selects their preferred language in Settings, and reminders are sent in that language automatically.
  • What languages are supported? Traditional Chinese, English, Filipino (Tagalog), Bahasa Indonesia.
